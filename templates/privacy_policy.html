Privacy Policy

We design with intention — and we treat your data the same way.

Last updated: 8 April 2026

At Licorice Locker, we respect your privacy.

This policy explains what we collect, how we use it, and what we don’t do with it.

What we collect

When you use our site, we may collect basic information such as:

  • Your name
  • Email address
  • Shipping and billing details
  • Basic site usage data (e.g. pages visited)

This information is only collected when necessary — for example, when you make a purchase or join The Listening Room.

How we use your information

We use your information to:

  • Process and deliver orders
  • Provide access to your Listening Room account
  • Track referrals, sales, and performance where applicable
  • Improve the experience of our website

We only use your data in ways that directly support these functions.

Listening Room privacy

If you join The Listening Room, your data remains private.

Other members do not have access to your personal information, sales data, or account details.

Each account is isolated and secure.

What we don’t do

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal data to third-party data brokers or advertisers.

Your information is not used for external marketing networks.

Third-party services

We may use trusted third-party services (such as payment processors or analytics tools) to operate our website.

These services only receive the data required to perform their function and are expected to handle it securely.

Cookies

We use cookies to:

  • Track Listening Room referrals
  • Understand basic website behaviour

These cookies help the site function properly and improve performance.

Security

We take reasonable steps to protect your information and keep it secure.

However, no system is completely immune to risk, and we encourage users to take care with their own data as well.
