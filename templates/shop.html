Liquorice Locker is where music becomes art — a design-led collection of minimalist vinyl record displays inspired by architecture, album artwork, and the emotional power of sound. Created by designer Joshua French, each piece is made to let the record take centre stage, turning the albums you love into sculptural pieces that shape the atmosphere of a room. Instead of hiding music in crates or shelves, Liquorice Locker brings it into the open, where colour, typography, and soundwave artwork become part of your space and part of your identity. Designed with simplicity, precision, and timeless materials, each display doesn’t compete with the music — it frames it.
