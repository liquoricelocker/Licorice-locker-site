Currency & international pricing

We display local pricing where possible, while processing securely in NZD.

Guide rates reflect approximate market levels; they change every business day.

All prices on our website are listed in New Zealand Dollars (NZD).

Automatic conversion

For international customers, prices may be displayed in your local currency (such as USD or EUR) for convenience.

This conversion is based on current exchange rates, which we update regularly. Rates move with global markets, so any converted amount is an estimate, not a locked price in that currency.

Exchange rates

Exchange rates fluctuate daily based on global currency markets.

As a general guide (approximate, for shopping context only):

  • 1 NZD ≈ 0.58 USD
  • 1 NZD ≈ 0.497 EUR

Please note that these are approximate rates and may vary slightly at the time of purchase.

Final payment amount

All transactions are processed in NZD.

Your bank or payment provider will convert the amount into your local currency at their exchange rate at settlement time.

This means the final amount charged may differ slightly from the displayed estimate.

Additional fees

Some banks or payment providers may apply international transaction or currency conversion fees.

These fees are not controlled by Licorice Locker.

Transparency

We aim to provide clear and accurate pricing for all customers.
